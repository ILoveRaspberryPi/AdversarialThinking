#Hardening-Labs README
This section documents my hands-on experience in securing operating systems and applications against unauthorized access and exploitation in accordance with modern standards and frameworks.

### Frameworks & Standards
  * **NIST CSF & ISO/IEC 27001:** Aligning technical controls with global security frameworks.
  * **The CIA Triad:** Ensuring the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of system resources through proactive defense.
  * **Zero Trust Principles:** Minimizing the attack surface by disabling unnecessary services and restricting permissions.
